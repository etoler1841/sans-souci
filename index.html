Sans Souci

Pensacola Beach, FL

About

White sand beach and crystal clear blue ocean are just outside your door for long beachside strolls or for feeling waves lap across your body. Watch for dolphins while lounging on your private covered extended beachfront balcony.

Enjoy a dip in the large shared beach side pool or relax in the shared hot tub. Sans Souci is a gated condominium and offers an air conditioned shared laundry facility with multiple washers and dryers, a reserved covered parking space just steps to the elevator, a gated open parking lot, and a convenience store with a DVD rental box and popular restaurant/bar Peg Leg Pete's with live entertainment directly across the street. Peg Leg Pete's is a family oriented restaurant and has a playground for young children with adjacent casual outdoor dining. Shopping, water sport rentals and more restaurants and bars are a just few blocks away. The Pensacola Naval Air Museum is another popular attraction in the area.

With the gulf and beach at your front door and the bay at your back door, this is the perfect place to soothe your soul while offering numerous entertainment options for activity seekers.

Come enjoy our beautiful beach and sunsets! Contact us today for questions and reservations.
